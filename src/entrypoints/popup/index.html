SC Bridge Sync

This extension reads your RSI hangar data and sends it to scbridge.app to keep your fleet in sync.

What we access:

  • Your RSI authentication cookie (used only for API calls)
  • Pledge data: ships, insurance, skins, items
  • CCU/upgrade history
  • Custom ship names
  • Account metadata (concierge level, org, balances)

What we do NOT do:

  • Store your RSI credentials or session token
  • Make purchases or modify your hangar
  • Share your data with third parties

By continuing, you agree to our Privacy Policy.
